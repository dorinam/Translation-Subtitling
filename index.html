About me


I started translating, subtitling and audio describing in 2014 in two projects at UNED while studying a Bachelor in Tourism in Spain.

  • I-AMICO: Initiative for Multilingual and Multimodal Information Access and Communication – Translation & Subtitling from English to Spanish, within the Masters Degree in Information Technology & Communication in Education and Treatment of Languages (TIC-ETL)
  • Records Project: Audio Description for visually impaired people in English

Since then I have been collaborating in several projects:

  • Coursera Global Translator Community as Lead Translator
  • Stanford University Entrepeneurship Corner
  • PBS News Hour
  • Scientific American

For the technical part of poker I have translated important authors such as:

  • Tournament material by Bencb
  • Cash material by YourDoom
  • Cash material by W34z3l
  • Cash material by Oblioo

Certified by: UNED,
VIC University & Coursera




With studies in several fields, such as translation, full stack web development, tourism, commerce and business, my areas of expertise are: education, business, travel, technology, gambling & sports betting.
